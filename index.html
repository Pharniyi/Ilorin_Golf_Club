A Legacy of Excellence

Founded to be the heart of golf in Kwara State, Ilorin Golf Club blends a proud heritage with modern facilities. Our 18-hole championship course offers a challenging yet enjoyable experience for every skill level.

From beginner lessons at our Academy to elite tournaments, the club fosters a welcoming community for players, families and corporate guests. Explore where tradition meets excellence.
