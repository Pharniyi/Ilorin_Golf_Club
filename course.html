The Course

Challenge, Learn and Perfect Your Game

Course Overview

Welcome to our meticulously maintained 18-hole, par-72 championship course. Spanning over 6,800 yards of lush, undulating terrain, our course offers a memorable round for golfers of all abilities. With strategically placed bunkers, challenging water hazards, and pristine greens, a day at Ilorin Golf Club is a day well spent.

Golf Academy

Improve your game with professional instruction at the Ilorin Golf Club Academy. We offer programs for all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned players, with individual and group lessons available.

Practice Facilities

Hone your skills at our state-of-the-art practice facilities, featuring a full-length driving range, pristine putting greens, and dedicated chipping areas to perfect every aspect of your game.

  • Chipping & Bunker Area
  • Large Putting Green
